View on GitHub View on PowerBI

Project Overview

A comprehensive exploration of music tracks that were released during a variety of years is presented in this dashboard on analysis. My goal is to provide useful details about how music has changed over time, to highlight standout tracks, and to find trends that have influenced the industry of music.

Key Features

  • used HTML to show off lively covers of the most streamed songs.
  • Focused on different timeframes, observe the evolution of song popularity throughout the year.
  • Uncoverd the distinctive musical vibe that filled these tracks.
  • utilized the dynamic TopN slicer to recognize the dominant streamers of the charts over different time period.

Key Learning

Background making through PowerPoint, Implemented a Deneb Heatmap, Deneb Unitchart, HTML Visual, Dynamic Slicer Selection with parameter.
