Pizza Sales Dashboard

View Power BI Dashboard View on GitHub

Project Overview

Conducted a pizza sales report challenge from Maven Analytics.I'm supposed to be a business intelligence consultant employed by for a Greek-inspired pizza shop in New Jersey, focusing on data analysis to enhance operations. The restaurant hired me to assist in using data to boost operations. Analyzed a real-world business case for a Pizza restaurant to enhance the restaurant's operations.

Key Insights

  • Analyzed over 50,000 records to identify patterns in customer demand, operational bottlenecks, and efficiency improvements.
  • Constructed ETL pipelines, transforming sales data into Power BI insights with advanced DAX measures.
  • Utilized Microsoft Power BI and MSSQL for data processing and visualization.
  • Constructed ETL pipelines to transform sales data into actionable insights.
  • Employed Power Query for data extraction, transformation, and loading.
  • Developed a dynamic dashboard displaying 15+ KPIs, including sales trends and customer demographics.
  • Addressed 5 key business questions on inventory management, peak hours, and operations to improve efficiency.
  • Recommended 7 key improvements projected to increase revenue by 3%, optimizing inventory, labor, and service.

Key Findings

  • Busiest Days and Times: Peak order volume occurs at 12 PM & 1 PM (lunch) and 5 PM - 6 PM (evening), with Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays being the busiest days.
  • Pizzas Made During Peak Periods: On Thursdays at noon, the restaurant produces 2,520 pizzas.
  • Best and Worst-Selling Pizzas: The best-selling pizza is the Classic Delux Pizza, while the worst is the Brie Carre Pizza.
  • Average Order Value: The average order value is approximately $38.30.
  • Proposed seven strategic improvements projected to increase revenue by 3%, focusing on optimizing inventory, labor, and service.
