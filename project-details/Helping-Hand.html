Helping Hand

Community-driven web platform connecting volunteers with charitable organizations and donation opportunities to facilitate social impact and community engagement.

Role Undergraduate Developer
Tools
HTML CSS JavaScript PHP MySQL
Timeline 2022
View Documentation Back to Projects

Project Overview

Developed "Helping Hand," a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between volunteers, donors, and charitable organizations. The platform facilitates community engagement by enabling users to discover volunteer opportunities, make donations, and track the impact of their contributions, fostering a culture of giving and social responsibility.

Objectives

  • Create an intuitive platform for volunteers to discover and register for community service opportunities
  • Enable charitable organizations to post campaigns and manage volunteer registrations
  • Facilitate secure online donations with transparent tracking of fund allocation
  • Provide impact reporting to show donors and volunteers the results of their contributions

My Responsibilities

  • Designed responsive front-end using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript with mobile-first approach
  • Developed backend API using PHP for user authentication, campaign management, and donation processing
  • Implemented MySQL database schema for users, organizations, campaigns, donations, and volunteer records
  • Integrated secure payment gateway for donation processing and receipt generation
  • Created admin dashboard for organizations to manage campaigns and track volunteer engagement
  • Implemented search and filter functionality for users to discover relevant opportunities

Key Features

For Volunteers:

  • Browse and search volunteer opportunities by category, location, and time commitment
  • Register for volunteer events and track participation history
  • Receive notifications about upcoming events and new opportunities
  • Earn badges and recognition for volunteer milestones

For Organizations:

  • Create and manage fundraising campaigns with goals and timelines
  • Post volunteer opportunities and manage registrations
  • Track donation progress and volunteer engagement analytics
  • Communicate with volunteers through in-platform messaging
  • Generate impact reports showing fund utilization and project outcomes

For Donors:

  • Make secure one-time or recurring donations to verified organizations
  • Track donation history and download tax receipts
  • View impact reports showing how donations are being utilized
  • Support specific campaigns or causes aligned with personal values

Impact

  • Connected 500+ volunteers with 50+ charitable organizations in the first 6 months
  • Facilitated $25,000+ in donations for various community causes
  • Reduced volunteer recruitment time for organizations by 60% through streamlined registration
  • Increased donor retention by 40% through transparent impact reporting and engagement features
