About Me

I'm a Data Analyst with a software engineering background, passionate about transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable business insights. I work with SQL, Looker Studio, Power BI, BigQuery, Excel, and Python to turn raw data into meaningful stories.

My experience spans ETL workflows, database management, data modeling, and interactive dashboard design, helping stakeholders monitor performance, uncover trends, and act faster.
